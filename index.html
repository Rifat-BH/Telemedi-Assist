About TeleMedi Assist

TeleMedi Assist is an end to end health solution that enables better health and wellbeing with advance technology.

TeleMedi Assist record users' problem details during appointments and generate comprehensive documentation. The documtent sent to doctors along with appointment schedules, facilitating quicker understanding of patient issues.

TeleMedi Assist enable patients to capture images of their eyes, tongue, and skin diseases during consultations. Utilize a machine learning algorithm to process captured images and identify potential diseases in real time. Provide doctors with high-quality images and suggested disease identifications, aiding in accurate diagnosis and treatment decision-making.
